Breaking it All Down

This amazing beef patty will be your absolute go-to at your next tailgate, cookout, or family get-together. I promise by the time you make these, everyone will be going back for seconds!

The bun is a homemade Brioche bun toasted to perfection. The beef patty is made of 80/20 angus ground beef. Topped with ooey gooey melted cheese and a Sriracha Mayo. Make sure to grill some onions and add some pickles!

Ingredients:

  • Brioche Buns
  • 80/20 Ground Beef
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sweet Onions
  • Dill Pickle Slices
  • Sriracha
  • Mayo

Steps:

  1. Form the ground beef into patties
  2. Grill the patties until they have reached your desired doneness
  3. Prep your vegetables
  4. Grill your onions
  5. Toast the Bread
  6. Mix the Sriracha and Mayo together
  7. Assemble!
