Amazing Fried Rice

The Basics

This simple recipe is a staple of many Asian-inspired dishes. Loaded with plenty of fresh vegetables, this fried rice is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Eggs
  • Scallions
  • Yellow Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  1. Make sure your rice is at least a day old (it works much better)!
  2. Fry the rice
  3. Crack the egg in the rice as it's frying
  4. Add the green onions and yellow onion
  5. Add rice vinegar, soy sauce, and sesame oil
